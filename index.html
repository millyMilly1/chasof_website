Who We Are

The Charles Soares Obiorah Foundation (CHASOF) Head Start ECD Intervention Library is a non-profit organization dedicated to early childhood development, education, and empowerment. CHASOF is committed to providing young children, particularly those in underserved communities, with access to books, learning resources, and educational support during their formative years.

Join Us In Shaping Lives

Our program is an ongoing Early Childhood Development initiative where registered children aged 0 to 5 years receive books bi-monthly. We aim to engage volunteers as home visitors or encourage parents to read to their children. Registration has begun in Ibusa town, Delta State, with 100 children already enrolled for our pilot phase. Our goal is to provide each child an average of 12 books per year, at approximately ₦5,000 per book, totaling ₦30,000 per child annually, including delivery and volunteer incentives.

Our Goals

We aim to empower young children in underserved communities by providing access to quality education, fostering literacy, and supporting their holistic development. Our goals include reaching 10,000 children annually, establishing 100 community libraries, and training 500 educators over the next five years.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of background, has the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive through early childhood education and community support.

Our Mission

To provide educational resources, books, and programs that inspire curiosity, build confidence, and lay the foundation for lifelong learning in children aged 0-8, with a focus on underserved regions.
